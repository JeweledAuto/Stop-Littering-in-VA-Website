Help Us Stop Littering in VA

Littering In Virginia has been steadily increasing over the years prompting much environmental concern and loss of visual appeal throughout the state.

We encourage you to leverage the resources on this site to educate yourself on the harms of littering and how you can help prevent and clean up littering in your local community.
